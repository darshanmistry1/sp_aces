How does it work

01
Find a little Sp_ACE.

Search for someone’s little Sp_ACE and check out how many owners are allowed and the cost.

02
Buy the Sp_ACE

When you’re ready pay by card. You’ll receive your owner code by email. Keep it safe, it acts as a key to the drop.

03
The exciting part!

What will be dropped?! Once a year, at a time of their choosing, the person / brand will drop something unique into their little Sp_ACE. The drop can only be accessed by the owner or co-owners of the little Sp_ACE.
